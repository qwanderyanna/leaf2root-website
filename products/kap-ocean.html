About This Workbook

The KAP Ocean Integration Workbook is designed to support the full arc of ketamine-assisted psychotherapy, from preparation through integration. The ocean theme weaves throughout, creating a cohesive visual and conceptual framework that mirrors the flowing, wave-like nature of the ketamine experience.

Each section has been carefully crafted by licensed clinicians with extensive experience in psychedelic-assisted therapy, ensuring both clinical relevance and client accessibility.

How to Use This Workbook

For Clinicians:

  • Use as a structured tool for KAP preparation sessions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this workbook only for ketamine therapy?

A: While designed specifically for KAP, the integration exercises can be adapted for other psychedelic-assisted therapies or transformative experiences.

Q: What's the difference between the Ocean and Celestial versions?

A: Both workbooks contain the same therapeutic content, but feature different visual themes. The Ocean version uses water and wave motifs, while the Celestial version uses space and star imagery. Choose based on what resonates more with you or your clients.

Q: How do I access the bonus digital resources?

A: Scan the QR code printed inside your workbook, or visit the product resources page on our website.

Q: Can I use this in my clinical practice?

A: Yes! This workbook is designed for use in clinical settings. You may purchase copies for your clients or recommend they purchase their own.
