About This Card Deck

Many people struggle to identify and name their emotions beyond "good," "bad," or "fine." This deck helps develop emotional granularity - the ability to identify specific emotions with precision.

Research shows that people who can identify their emotions with greater specificity are better at regulating those emotions and have improved mental health outcomes.

What's Included

Each card includes:

  • Emotion name and definition
  • Common body sensations associated with this emotion
  • Related emotions (similar/different)
  • Reflection prompt for deeper exploration

Emotions include: Joy, contentment, excitement, pride, gratitude, hope, love, curiosity, awe, serenity, sadness, grief, disappointment, loneliness, heartache, melancholy, anger, frustration, irritation, resentment, rage, fear, anxiety, worry, dread, nervousness, shame, guilt, embarrassment, vulnerability, and many more.

How to Use This Deck

For Therapists:

  • Use as a feelings check-in at the start of session
  • Help clients identify and name emotions
  • Explore the body sensation connection

For Families:

  • Daily feelings check-in at dinner
  • Help children develop emotional vocabulary
  • Normalize the full range of emotions

For Personal Use:

  • Build your emotional vocabulary
  • Use for journaling prompts
  • Improve emotional self-awareness

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What age is this appropriate for?

A: The cards can be used with children as young as 6-7 (with adult guidance) through adults. Some emotions are more complex than others.

Q: Are there "negative" emotions in the deck?

A: All emotions are included! We don't label emotions as "good" or "bad" - all emotions are valid and have information to offer us.
