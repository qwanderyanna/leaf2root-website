About This Workbook

Internal Family Systems (IFS) is a powerful therapeutic model that views the mind as naturally multiple - made up of different parts with different feelings, beliefs, and roles. This workbook helps you explore your own internal system with curiosity and compassion.

Whether you're working with an IFS therapist or exploring on your own, this workbook provides structure for getting to know your parts and developing Self-leadership.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are "parts"?

A: In IFS, parts are the different aspects of our psyche - like the inner critic, the anxious part, the people-pleaser, etc. They're not disorders, but natural aspects of being human.

Q: Can I do IFS work on my own?

A: Basic parts awareness and Self-energy practice can be done independently. For deeper work, especially with wounded parts (exiles), working with a trained IFS therapist is recommended.
