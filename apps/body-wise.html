Body Wise

Mindful somatic practices for body awareness and regulation

About Body Wise

Body Wise is a comprehensive somatic therapy toolkit designed to help clinicians and clients develop greater body awareness, emotional regulation, and trauma-informed healing practices.

This evidence-based tool integrates principles from Somatic Experiencing, Sensorimotor Psychotherapy, and mindfulness-based interventions to support gentle, compassionate reconnection with the body.

Grounding Exercises

Guided practices to help clients feel safe and present in their bodies.

Body Scan Meditations

Progressive awareness exercises for noticing sensations without judgment.

Trauma-Informed

All practices designed with safety and client empowerment at the forefront.

Progress Tracking

Track client engagement and response to somatic interventions over time.
