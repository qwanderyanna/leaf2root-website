Frequently Asked Questions

How long will founding pricing last?

We're offering founding member pricing during our early access phase to recognize and thank the people who support us as we grow. As the platform matures and expands, pricing will eventually adjust to reflect the increased value and operational costs. We'll provide 2-3 weeks notice before any changes, and anyone subscribed before then will keep their founding rate while their subscription remains active.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You have complete autonomy over your subscription. Cancel anytime through your account settings, no questions asked.

Are my tools and reflections private?

Absolutely. Your personal work is yours alone. We use privacy-first design principles and will never share your activity, reflections, or usage patterns with anyone.

What's the difference between personal and professional subscriptions?

Personal subscriptions (what you're viewing now) are for individuals using tools for themselves. Professional subscriptions are for licensed mental health professionals who use tools in clinical practice with clients. They serve different purposes and have different features. Learn about professional subscriptions.

Need help deciding?

Contact us with questions. We're here to support you in finding what works for your journey.
