Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
Yes! We offer discounted pricing for practices, schools, and organizations ordering 10+ units of any product. Contact us with details about your needs and we'll provide a custom quote. Bulk licensing for digital products is also available.
Can I use your resources with my clients?
Absolutely. All our products are designed for therapeutic use. Single purchases include a personal or single-practitioner license. For multi-clinician practices, please inquire about our practice-wide licensing options.
What's your return policy?
Physical products can be returned within 30 days of purchase if unused and in original condition. Digital downloads are non-refundable, but if you're unsatisfied, contact us and we'll work to make it right.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship physical products within the United States. However, all our digital downloads are available worldwide immediately upon purchase.
How do I access digital downloads after purchase?
After your purchase is complete, you'll receive an email with a download link. Downloads are available immediately and the link remains active for 30 days. If you have trouble accessing your files, contact us at orders@leaf2root.org.
Are your resources evidence-based?
Yes. Every Leaf2Root product is developed by licensed clinicians and grounded in proven therapeutic approaches including CBT, DBT, EMDR, and somatic therapies. We test all resources in actual clinical settings before offering them for sale.
