Leaf2Root was born from a simple observation: the most effective therapeutic tools often weren't available outside the therapy room.

We're changing that by creating evidence-based mental health resources that clinicians can trust and individuals can use independently.

Our Story

From Therapy Room to Resource Library

As clinicians at Family Solutions Counseling, we found ourselves constantly creating custom worksheets, card exercises, and homework materials for our clients. Time and again, we'd see the same needs arise: a grounding exercise that actually worked, a thought record that clients would actually complete, tools that bridged the gap between sessions.

Our clients kept asking where they could buy the materials we'd made. They wanted to share them with family members, keep them accessible at home, use them between appointments. That's when we realized these resources could help far more people than just those walking through our doors.

"Every resource we create is something we've used successfully with real clients. If it doesn't work in the therapy room, it doesn't make it to the shop."

Leaf2Root represents our philosophy: sustainable healing grows from the ground up. Just as a plant needs strong roots to flourish, mental wellness requires foundational skills, consistent practice, and the right tools. Our name reminds us—and our community—that growth is a process, not an event.

Today, we develop resources that serve two distinct but equally important audiences: mental health professionals seeking quality clinical tools, and individuals looking for accessible self-help resources. Every product reflects our commitment to evidence-based approaches, inclusive design, and practical usability.

Our Parent Company

A Division of Family Solutions Counseling

Leaf2Root is proudly part of Family Solutions Counseling, a Utah-based mental health practice founded in 2017. Our parent company provides therapy services throughout Utah and Idaho, serving individuals, couples, and families with evidence-based, affirming care.

Family Solutions specializes in a wide range of therapeutic approaches including EMDR, DBT, CBT, IFS, and ART. We're an all-inclusive, neurodivergent and LGBTQ+ affirming practice committed to innovative, research-based treatment.

Every Leaf2Root resource is developed and reviewed by licensed clinicians from Family Solutions, ensuring that what we sell reflects what actually works in therapeutic practice.

Our Values

What Guides Our Work

Every resource we create is shaped by these core principles.

Evidence-Based

We only create tools grounded in proven therapeutic approaches. Every exercise, worksheet, and card deck reflects research-backed methods from modalities like CBT, DBT, EMDR, and somatic therapies.

Inclusive by Design

Mental health resources should work for everyone. We use affirming language, diverse representation, and accessible design to ensure our tools serve all identities and backgrounds.

Clinically Tested

Every product has been used successfully in real therapeutic settings. Our team at Family Solutions tests and refines resources with actual clients before we ever offer them for sale.

Accessible Pricing

Quality mental health tools shouldn't require a premium price tag. We keep costs reasonable and offer digital versions so resources remain accessible to individuals and small practices alike.

Practical Usability

Beautiful design means nothing if a resource isn't easy to use. We prioritize clear instructions, intuitive layouts, and materials that work seamlessly in session or at home.

Continuous Learning

Mental health treatment evolves, and so do we. We continuously update our resources based on new research, client feedback, and advances in therapeutic practice.
