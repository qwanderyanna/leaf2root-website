Understanding Professional Subscriptions

What does "active client" mean?

An active client is someone with a currently valid access code you've generated. Codes expire every 3 months and must be regenerated to reflect active therapeutic relationships. This approach honors the natural rhythm of clinical work without invasive monitoring.

What's included in the subscription?

Your professional subscription includes access to ALL tools (both professional-facing and client-facing), the ability to generate client access codes, and clinical integration features. You're not just paying for professional toolsâ€”you're also paying for the infrastructure that supports your clients' access.

How do client access codes work?

  • You generate unique codes through your professional dashboard
  • Codes grant your clients access to client-level tools (not professional tools)
  • Codes expire every 3 months to reflect active therapeutic relationships
  • You maintain control over access by regenerating codes as needed
  • Clients cannot see your professional analytics or clinical controls

What if I exceed my client limit?

If you reach your client capacity, we'll display a calm explanation and offer options to regenerate codes or consider upgrading. We won't lock your account or make accusationsâ€”limits exist as sustainable capacity boundaries, not punitive measures.

Professional Responsibility & Ethical Use

As a licensed mental health professional, you understand that these tools are designed to complement clinical treatment, not replace it. Professional subscriptions grant you stewardship over client access within your therapeutic relationships.

Your subscription includes tools for both in-session use and between-session support. We trust your clinical judgment in how and when to integrate these resources into treatment.

We do not monitor individual client activity or usage patterns. Your clients' privacy and autonomy remain protected.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do professional plans cost more than client plans?

Professional subscriptions include not just professional tools, but also the infrastructure for client access, code generation systems, and capacity for multiple users. Each additional client increases our operational, security, and maintenance costs. Professional pricing reflects both the clinical value and the technical reality of supporting therapeutic relationships.

Can my clients subscribe directly instead?

Client subscriptions are for individuals using tools for personal growth outside of clinical treatment. They don't include professional permissions, analytics, or clinical integration features. If you're working with clients in a therapeutic context, professional subscriptions ensure proper role boundaries and ethical access control.

Do you monitor how I use tools with clients?

No. We trust your clinical judgment. You'll see your active client slot count, but we don't track individual client activity, usage patterns, or identify specific clients. Privacy and clinical autonomy are core to our design.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime through your account settings. Your active client codes will remain valid through their current 3-month period.

Questions about professional use?

Contact our clinical support team for guidance on integrating these tools into your practice.
